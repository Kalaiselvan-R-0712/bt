// OUR STORY

Building the Future of Biotechnology

Founded in 1998, Stackly Biotechnology Institute has grown into a global hub for multidisciplinary scientific innovation. Our laboratories combine advanced technologies with world-class expertise to deliver transformative discoveries in life sciences.

From molecular biology and genomics to environmental sustainability and pharmaceutical research, our mission is to develop solutions that improve human health and global wellbeing.

Our scientists collaborate with international research centers, universities, and industry partners to accelerate scientific breakthroughs.

// HISTORY

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped the Stackly Biotechnology Institute.

1978

The early scientific foundation of Stackly began with a small biotechnology research group focused on molecular biology and environmental microbiology.

1988

The research team expanded into advanced laboratory technologies including early genetic sequencing and pharmaceutical compound analysis.

1998

Stackly Biotechnology Institute officially founded with a vision to advance life science research through innovation and global collaboration.

2008

Expanded laboratories and launched major international collaborative research programs across multiple scientific disciplines.

2018

Developed breakthrough biotechnology platforms used in global pharmaceutical innovation and medical research with global power.

2026

Recognized as a leading research institute driving next-generation scientific discovery and biotechnology innovation worldwide.
